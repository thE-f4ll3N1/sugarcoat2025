Privacy Policy


At Sugarcoat Salon, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or engage with our services.


1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you visit our website or book a service:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Appointment details
  • Any messages or inquiries submitted via our contact forms
  • IP address and browser data (collected through cookies)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Confirm and manage appointments
  • Respond to inquiries or feedback
  • Send updates or promotional messages (only with your consent)

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • Your data may be shared with trusted partners like payment gateways or booking systems strictly to fulfill service requests.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

4. Cookies & Tracking

  • Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and collect anonymous analytics data.
  • You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online platform is 100% secure.


6. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You may opt-out of promotional messages by clicking "unsubscribe" or informing us directly.


7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Please review it periodically. Continued use of our site implies your acceptance of the revised policy.

Last Updated on 21 September 2025.


8. Contact Us

For any further privacy-related questions. You can contact us.
