Hair Smoothening in Hennur Road, Bangalore

Looking for Hair Smoothening in Hennur Road, Bangalore? Hair Smoothening is a professional treatment that reduces frizz, improves manageability, and gives your hair a sleek, polished appearance while maintaining a natural look. Clients with severely damaged hair may benefit from Hair Botox Treatment before opting for Hair Smoothening.

Benefits of hair smoothening

  • Reduces frizz and flyaways
  • Improves hair texture
  • Adds shine and softness
  • Makes hair easier to manage
  • Reduces styling time
  • Suitable for most hair types

Who Should Get Hair Smoothening?

  • Frizzy hair
  • Wavy and difficult-to-manage hair
  • Dry and rough hair
  • Hair exposed to humidity
  • People wanting smoother-looking hair

Hair Smoothening process

  • Step 1: Hair consultation
  • Step 2: Deep cleansing
  • Step 3: Smoothening cream application
  • Step 4: Heat sealing process
  • Step 5: Wash and styling

Duration: 2-4 Hours
Results last: Permanent (new growth will be original texture)

Hair Smoothening vs Keratin

Hair Smoothening focuses on controlling frizz and improving manageability while maintaining a natural appearance. Keratin treatments generally provide additional repair and protein replenishment. You can also explore our Keratin Treatment and Hair Botox Service for alternative hair transformation solutions.

Aftercare Tips

  • Use sulphate-free shampoo
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Follow professional aftercare advice
  • Use nourishing hair masks regularly
  • Periodic Hair Spa Treatments can help maintain smoothness and hydration.

FAQs – Hair Smoothening

Is Hair Smoothening safe?

Yes, when performed professionally.

How long does Hair Smoothening last?

Typically Hair smoothening is permanent. When new growth comes, those hair will be of original texture. Can be redone in 5-6 months for even look.

Can coloured hair be smoothened?

Yes, smoothening can be performed on coloured hair after professional assessment.
