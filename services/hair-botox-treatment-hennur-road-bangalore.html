Hair Botox Treatment in Hennur Road, Bangalore

Looking for Hair Botox Treatment in Hennur Road, Bangalore? Hair Botox is a deep-conditioning treatment that restores moisture, repairs damage, and smoothens frizzy hair without altering your natural texture.

What Is Hair Botox Treatment?

Hair Botox is a non-chemical hair rejuvenation treatment enriched with proteins, vitamins, amino acids, and nourishing oils. It repairs hair from within and improves softness, shine, and manageability

Benefits of Hair Botox

  • Visible reduction in frizz
  • Deep hydration for dry hair
  • Improved shine and smoothness
  • Repairs damage from heat & colouring
  • Safe for coloured and chemically treated hair

Who Should Get Hair Botox?

  • People with frizzy or rough hair
  • Dry, dull, or lifeless hair
  • Hair damaged due to colouring or styling
  • Those wanting smooth hair without straightening
  • People with frizzy or rough hair may also consider Hair Smoothening or Keratin Treatment depending on their desired results.

Hair Botox Treatment Process

  • Step 1: Hair cleansing
  • Step 2: Hair Botox application
  • Step 3: Heat activation
  • Step 4: Rinse & professional blow-dry

Duration: 2-2.5 hours
Results last: 3-5 months (with proper home care)

Hair Botox vs Keratin

Hair Botox focuses on repair and hydration, while keratin is a straightening treatment. If you want natural-looking smooth hair without chemicals, Hair Botox is ideal. Read more about Keratin vs Botox Treatment for hair. Compare Hair Botox with our Hair Smoothening Service to understand which treatment is best for your hair type.

Aftercare Tips

  • Use sulphate-free shampoo. Preferably for Botox / Keratin aftercare.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Avoid excessive oiling
  • Follow stylist-recommended products
  • Regular Hair Spa treatments can help maintain hydration and prolong results.

FAQs – Hair Botox in Hennur Road

Is Hair Botox safe for all hair types?

Yes, it’s safe and suitable for most hair types, including coloured hair.

Does Hair Botox straighten hair permanently?

No, it smoothens and reduces frizz without permanent straightening.

How long does Hair Botox last?

Results usually last 3–5 months depending on home hair care routine.
