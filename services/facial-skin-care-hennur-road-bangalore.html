Hydra Facial

Our Hydra Facial is a non-invasive skin treatment that combines deep cleansing, gentle exfoliation, extraction, hydration and antioxidant infusion in one session. It removes dead skin cells, unclogs pores and restores moisture, leaving your skin fresh, smooth and glowing without downtime.

  • Deep pore cleansing
  • Blackhead & whitehead removal
  • Instant skin hydration
  • Improves dull and tired skin
  • Suitable for oily, dry and combination skin
  • Minimal redness and no recovery time

Hydra Facial is ideal before weddings, parties, photoshoots and special occasions when you want immediate skin radiance.

Korean Facial

Inspired by Korean skincare techniques, our Korean Facial focuses on intense hydration, nourishment and skin barrier repair to achieve healthy, glass-like skin. The treatment improves skin texture while enhancing natural brightness and elasticity.

  • Deep hydration
  • Brightens dull complexion
  • Improves skin elasticity
  • Reduces rough texture
  • Promotes healthy glowing skin
  • Suitable for most skin types

Korean Facial is perfect for people experiencing dehydration, uneven skin tone, tired-looking skin or anyone wanting a naturally radiant complexion.

FAQs – Facial & Skin Care

What is the difference between Hydra Facial and Korean Facial?

Hydra Facial focuses on deep cleansing, exfoliation and hydration, while Korean Facial emphasises long-lasting hydration, nourishment and achieving naturally radiant, glass-like skin.

Which facial is best before a wedding or event?

Both are excellent choices. Hydra Facial delivers immediate skin freshness, while Korean Facial provides a hydrated, luminous finish that's ideal before special occasions.

How often should I get a Hydra Facial?

For healthy skin maintenance, once every 4–6 weeks is generally recommended.

Are these treatments suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. Our experts analyse your skin beforehand and customise the treatment using products suitable for your skin type.

Are facials suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, treatments are customised based on skin type. And we do have specialized facials for sensitive skins.

How often should I get a facial?

Every 3–4 weeks for best results.

Can men opt for facial treatments?

Yes, facials are suitable for both men and women.
