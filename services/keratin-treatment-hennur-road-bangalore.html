Keratin Hair Treatment in Hennur Road, Bangalore

Keratin Hair Treatment is a professional smoothening treatment that reduces frizz, straightens hair, and restores lost keratin—making hair soft, manageable, and shiny.

What Is Keratin Hair Treatment?

Keratin treatment replenishes natural keratin protein in the hair shaft and seals the cuticle using heat. This helps reduce curls, waves, and frizz while improving hair texture and shine.

Benefits of Keratin Treatment

  • Long-lasting smooth and straight hair
  • Reduces frizz and volume
  • Makes hair soft and manageable
  • Adds shine and polish
  • Ideal for thick, curly, or wavy hair

Who Should Opt for Keratin Treatment?

  • Frizzy or unmanageable hair
  • Curly or wavy hair
  • Hair damaged by heat or colouring
  • Anyone wanting straight-looking hair

Keratin Treatment Process

  • Step 1: Deep cleansing shampoo
  • Step 2: Keratin product application
  • Step 3: Heat sealing using flat iron
  • Step 4: Final rinse and styling

Duration: 2-3 hours
Results last: 5-7 months (with proper home care)

Keratin vs Hair Botox

Keratin is a straightening and smoothing treatment, while Hair Botox focuses on repair and hydration. Keratin is ideal if you want long-lasting straight hair. Read more about Keratin vs Botox Treatment for hair.

Aftercare Tips for Keratin

  • Use sulphate-free shampoo. Preferably for Keratin aftercare.
  • Avoid frequent washing
  • Limit heat styling
  • Follow stylist-recommended products

FAQs – Keratin Treatment in Hennur Road

Is keratin treatment safe?

Yes, when done professionally and with proper aftercare.

Does keratin permanently straighten hair?

No, it’s semi-permanent relaxed smooth look and gradually fades.

How long does keratin treatment last?

Typically 3–6 months depending on hair care routine.
