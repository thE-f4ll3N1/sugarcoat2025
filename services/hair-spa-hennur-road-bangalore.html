Hair Spa in Hennur Road, Bangalore

Looking for Hair Spa in Hennur Road, Bangalore? Our professional Hair Spa treatments help nourish the scalp, strengthen hair, reduce dryness, and improve overall hair health.

Benefits of hair spa

  • Deep nourishment
  • Improved scalp health
  • Reduced dryness
  • Better hair texture
  • Enhanced shine
  • Relaxing experience
  • Hair Spa is an excellent maintenance treatment after Keratin, Hair Botox, Hair Colour or Hair Smoothening.

Who Should Get Hair Spa?

  • Dry hair
  • Damaged hair
  • Chemically treated hair
  • Dull and lifeless hair
  • People experiencing scalp dryness
  • People with coloured hair should also explore our Hair Colour Services.

Hair Spa process

  • Step 1: Hair and scalp analysis
  • Step 2: Cleansing
  • Step 3: Hair Spa cream application (with ampules/plex)
  • Step 4: Steam treatment(according to scalp)
  • Step 5: Rinse and drying

Duration: 60-90 minutes
Recommended frequency: Every 2-4 weeks

Hair Spa vs Hair Botox

Hair Spa focuses on regular nourishment and scalp health, while Hair Botox is a deeper hair-repair treatment designed to reduce frizz and improve hair structure.

Aftercare Tips

  • Use gentle shampoos
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Follow a healthy hair-care routine
  • Schedule regular hair spa sessions

FAQs – Hair Spa

How often should I get a Hair Spa?

Usually every 2–4 weeks. Regular Hair Spa treatments can help maintain hydration and prolong results.

Does Hair Spa reduce hair fall?

It may help improve scalp health, which supports healthier hair.

Can Hair Spa be done on coloured hair?

Yes.
