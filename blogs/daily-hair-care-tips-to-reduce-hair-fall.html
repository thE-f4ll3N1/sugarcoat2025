Daily Hair Care Habits That Actually Reduce Hair Fall in Bangalore Weather

Written by

Mahina Sehrish
Sugarcoat Salon, Bangalore

Date: 12 Jan 2026

Why Hair Fall Is So Common in Bangalore

Hair fall is one of the most common concerns we hear at salons, especially in cities like Bangalore where weather, water quality, and lifestyle all play a role. While some hair fall is normal, the right daily habits can significantly reduce excessive shedding and breakage.

Cleanse Your Scalp the Right Way

A clean scalp is the foundation of healthy hair growth.

Best practices:

  • Wash your hair 2–3 times a week
  • Use a mild, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Always dilute shampoo before applying

Oil Smart, Not Heavy

Oiling helps, but only when done correctly.

What works:

  • Light oiling once or twice a week
  • Gentle massage for 5–10 minutes
  • Wash off within a few hours

Don’t Skip Conditioner

Skipping conditioner is a common mistake. Conditioner or a good hair mask can be used after shampooing. Apply on mid-lengths and ends.

Conditioner helps to:

  • Reduce breakage
  • Improve manageability
  • Protect hair length

Reduce Heat and Tight Hairstyles

Frequent heat styling and tight hairstyles stress the hair roots.

To minimise damage:

  • Limit straighteners and blow dryers
  • Avoid tight buns daily
  • Use heat protectant before styling

Eat Well and Stay Hydrated

Hair health starts from within.

Include:

  • Protein-rich foods
  • Iron and omega-3 sources
  • Adequate daily water intake

When to Seek Professional Help

If hair fall continues despite good care, professional analysis can help identify scalp or damage-related issues. At Sugarcoat salon, we have treatments to repair damaged hair and to keep the scalp healthy and clean.

Final Thoughts

Consistent daily care combined with professional guidance is the most effective way to control hair fall in the long run.
