Latest Gents Haircuts That Are Trending Right Now

Written by

Mahina Sehrish
Sugarcoat Salon, Bangalore

Date: 25 Jan 2026

Men’s grooming in 2026 is all about clean structure, natural texture, and low-maintenance styling. Whether you prefer sharp fades or relaxed, effortless looks, these trending gents haircuts are dominating salons right now.

The Overgrown Buzz Cut

The overgrown buzz cut is a modern take on the original buzz. It takes the simple classic and transforms it into a more modern style with slightly overgrown shape. It can also be dyed for added flavor or rocked with your normal hair to keep things simple. While it's normally considered a great style for the summer months, when paired with a beard it has the perfect lumberjack aesthetic to start out the cold 2026 Winter with.

Best For:

Casual and Professional looks. For those who prefer less maintenance and easy styling.

How to Style:

Here’s the best part: you basically don’t. The overgrown buzz cut is extremely low-maintenance. What is important is keeping your hair and scalp clean. Use a high-quality shampoo regularly to wash away oils, sweat, and dirt. Lather CLEAN through your hair in the shower for a refreshing, invigorating scalp cleanse. Pair it with the Scalp Massage Brush for extra exfoliation.

The Textured Crop

2025 was all about texture. We think that trend is just getting started. The textured crop continues to be a top choice for men who want a stylish yet easy-to-maintain haircut. With choppy layers on top and a clean fade on the sides, this style works well for straight and wavy hair.

Best For:

Casual and professional settings. Ideal for men who want a trendy haircut with minimal daily styling.

How to Style:

Apply a small amount of matte clay or texture powder to dry or slightly damp hair. Use fingers to create separation and natural movement. Avoid heavy products to keep the look lightweight.

The Warrior Cut

The warrior cut once popularized by Brad Pitt, is a bold, masculine hairstyle defined by strong structure and controlled length. Inspired by rugged, confident looks, this cut usually features shorter sides with a powerful, textured top. It delivers a sharp appearance while still maintaining natural flow.

Best For:

Men who prefer strong, edgy styles and confident grooming aesthetics.

How to Style:

Blow-dry the top for lift and structure. Finish with a medium-hold matte product to enhance texture while keeping the look controlled throughout the day.

Curly Hair Fade

The curly hair fade is designed to highlight natural curls while keeping the sides clean and sharp. The fade enhances contrast, allowing the curls on top to stand out while maintaining a fresh and polished appearance.

Best For:

Men with naturally curly or wavy hair who want a neat yet expressive hairstyle.

How to Style:

Use a curl-enhancing cream or light leave-in conditioner. Scrunch gently and allow hair to air-dry or diffuse to maintain curl definition without frizz.

The Tousled Wave

The tousled wave is a relaxed, natural hairstyle that embraces movement and texture. This cut focuses on medium length with soft layering, giving the hair a slightly messy but intentional finish that looks effortless.

Best For:

Casual wear and everyday styling. Ideal for men who prefer a laid-back appearance.

How to Style:

Apply sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch lightly. Let it air-dry for a natural wave pattern, or use a diffuser for added volume.

Burst Fade

The burst fade is a standout haircut where the fade curves around the ear, creating a circular burst effect. It adds dimension and sharpness while allowing flexibility in styling the top.

Best For:

Trend-focused men who want a modern and eye-catching haircut.

How to Style:

Style the top based on hair type—textured, curly, or slick. Use a lightweight styling product to maintain definition without overpowering the fade.

The Flow

The flow haircut is all about length, movement, and confidence. With longer layers swept back or to the side, this style delivers a natural, effortless look that continues to trend strongly in 2026.

Best For:

Men with medium to long hair who prefer a relaxed, stylish appearance.

How to Style:

Use a light styling cream or leave-in conditioner. Comb back loosely or let hair fall naturally for a soft, flowing finish.

Modern Taper Fade

The modern taper fade offers a clean and refined look with subtle blending around the temples and neckline. Unlike sharp fades, this style delivers a more natural transition that suits both formal and casual environments.

Best For:

Office professionals and men seeking a polished everyday haircut.

How to Style:

Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to the top. Keep the finish natural with minimal shine.

Mid Fade with Natural Top

The mid fade with a natural top strikes a perfect balance between clean sides and relaxed styling. The fade starts midway on the head, keeping the look sharp while allowing the top to maintain natural texture and volume.

Best For:

Men looking for a versatile haircut that suits most face shapes and hair types.

How to Style:

Use a light matte product and style with fingers. Keep the top natural for a clean, modern finish.
